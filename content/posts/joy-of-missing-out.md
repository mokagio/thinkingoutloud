Nassim Taleb wrote:

> You are rich if money you refuse tastes better than money you accept.

I've been feeling something similar recently when skipping podcast episodes from shows I used to follow closely.
Whenever I say to myself, "I can do better than listen to this," I implicitly enrich my mental life.

I used to experience _FOMO_, fear of missing out on a useful tip or a novel take.
These days, I mostly feel _JOMO_, joy of missing out on input that does not nourish.
